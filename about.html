Our Story

20 Years of Building Dreams

Founded in 2005 by Mr. Selvam Rajan in the heart of Kovilpatti, our company began as a small team of passionate builders determined to change the face of construction in the Thoothukudi district.

Today, we are Tamil Nadu's most trusted mid-scale construction company, with over 500 completed projects, a team of 500+ skilled professionals, and a reputation that speaks for itself.

Our growth has been built on three pillars: Quality, Integrity, and Innovation. We don't just build structures — we build lasting relationships with every client.
