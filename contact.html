Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical residential construction take? ▼
A typical 2BHK or 3BHK home (1200–2000 sqft) takes 12–18 months from foundation to handover, depending on design complexity, material availability, and weather conditions.
Do you provide a warranty on your construction? ▼
Yes! We provide a 5-year structural warranty and 1-year finish warranty on all our projects. Any defects during this period are rectified at no extra cost.
Can you help with building permits and government approvals? ▼
Absolutely. Our experienced team handles all permit filings, municipality approvals, and regulatory compliance — so you don't have to deal with any paperwork.
Do you take projects outside Kovilpatti? ▼
Yes, we take projects across the Thoothukudi district and other parts of Tamil Nadu. For large-scale projects, we can operate statewide. Contact us to discuss your location.
What is your payment structure? ▼
We follow a milestone-based payment schedule — advance, foundation completion, slab completion, finishing, and handover. This ensures transparency and aligns payments with visible progress.
